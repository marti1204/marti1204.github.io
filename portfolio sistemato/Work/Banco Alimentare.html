IMP(I)ATTARE NEL QUOTIDIANO

Project for the Polytechinc University of Turin in collaboration with Banco Alimentare Piemonte Onlus.

Group Project: Rocchietti Martina, Scabin Federico, Simoni Alessandro, Vaira Elena, Zanotti Ester,

Collection of 10 placements to be distributed within the universtity cafeteria throughout the academic year. These aim to inform and raise awareness among young students about food waste while also revealing interesting facts about the Piedmont region, where Banco Alimentare Onlus operates.
