#MESCOLIAMOCI

A COMBINATION OF DIVERSITY

Communication campaign project for the Polytechnic University of Turin in collaboration with the City of Turin.

Group Project: Rocchietti Martina, Xhumari Gabriel, Zanotti Ester

Colofrul or customizable cootie catchers designed to teach children aged 6 to 11 the importance of kindness and the meaning of the word peace. Through instructions provided below on the paper and on a separate sheet, teachers and children can easily understand how the cootie catcher works, promoting learning through play.
