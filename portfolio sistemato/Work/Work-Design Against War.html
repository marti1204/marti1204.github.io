Give Peace a Chance

FREE PRINTABLE COOTIE CATCHER

In collaboration with the Polytechnic University of Milan, a projects for the Polytechnic University of Turin for the Instagram page Desgin Against War.

Group Project: Rocchi Allegra, Romano Maria Giulia, Romeo Antonio, Rocchietti Martina



Colorful or customizable cootie catchers designed to teach children aged 6 to 11 the importance of kindness and the meaning of the word peace. Through instructions provided below on the paper and on a separate sheet, teachers and children can easily understand how the cootie catcher works, promoting learning through play.
