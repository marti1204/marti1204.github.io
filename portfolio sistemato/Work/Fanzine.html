BLACK AND WHITE (and a touch of color)

Editorial project for the Polytechnic University of Turin.

Individual project

Black and white, mundane as a theme, you might say. And you're right, it's the most commonplace topic imaginable. Yet, it's exactly what life is based on: highs and lows, fullness and emptiness. So, what does color have to do with it? Well, simple: the touch of color is what makes us human, imperfect and irrational. Without color, the world would be perfect, in complete balance, but we're not like that; we make mistakes, get angry and fall in love, all without following any logic. So yes, black and white is certainly the most commonplace there imaginable, but how could I not choose it?
